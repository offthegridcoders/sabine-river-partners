  • Real world predictive models
    Predictive modeling

    Predictive models can be one of our best planning and decision making tools. SRP specializes in real world, predictive models that inform critical decisions with confidence. Our philosophy is to take a practical approach to modeling, knowing that no data source is ever perfect, and that real world decisions still need to be made,. We tie predictive models to physical measurements to benchmark and refine modeling results. Confidence in decision making comes from the knowledge that modeling accurately reflects what is observed in the real world.

    Our decision critical models include:

    • Air dispersion/deposition modeling refined using air monitoring measurements
    • Coastal spill modeling incorporating shoreline geomorphology, time series aerial photography, and irregular field collection and sampling data
    • Soil to food modeling based on site specific soil characteristics and field measured uptake
    • Geochemical modeling benchmarked with soil boring data
    • Multi variate health outcome models using environmental, socioeconomic, and demographic data
    © 2015 Sabine River Partners

    Data Tools

    Sometimes the most important data tools are the ones we use to monitor key performance indicators every day. We balance front-end user experience with the strategic needs of the organization to build data tools that accomplishing the desired business outcome with an elegant user experience.

    Some examples include:

    • Tool for performing Statistical Quality Control (SQC) calculations and control charting for compliance with 40 CFR Part 80 Performance Based Analytical Test Method Approach, and consistent ASTM 6299 requirements
    • Software for uptake of asbestos testing NADES spreadsheets into a web based searchable tool that performs Poisson statistical calculations.
    • Online knowledge management library of historic research documents
    • Online tool to store and search (by location) microdata from EPA’s Risk Screening Environmental Indicators (RSEI) dataset detailing air modeling on a 1 km grid for sources across the US.
